# Notepad Editor Online - Free Browser-Based Text Editor

![Notepad Editor](https://notepadeditor.github.io/images/notepad-editor-banner.png)

> **A clean, fast, and completely private online notepad** that works instantly in your browser. No sign-up, no installation, no data collection.

**[🌐 Open Notepad Editor Now](https://notepadeditor.github.io/)**

---

## ✨ Features

This **free online notepad editor** offers everything you need for quick note-taking, writing, and document drafting:

- **Auto-Save** – Your work is automatically saved to your browser using `localStorage`
- **Live Statistics** – Real-time word count, character count, line count, sentence count, and estimated reading time
- **Find & Replace** – Powerful search and replace functionality
- **Case Converter** – Instantly convert text to UPPERCASE, lowercase, or Title Case
- **Export Options** – Download as `.txt` or `.md` (Markdown) files
- **Dark Mode** – Eye-friendly dark theme with one-click toggle
- **Focus / Distraction-Free Mode** – Full-screen writing experience
- **Responsive Design** – Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Line Numbers** – Optional line numbering for better readability
- **Keyboard Shortcuts** – Supports standard editing shortcuts
- **100% Private** – Everything stays in your browser. No servers, no accounts, no tracking.
