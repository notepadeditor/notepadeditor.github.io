Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 22, 2026

At Notepad Editor Online, available at notepadeditor.github.io, your privacy is our top priority. Because our text editor operates completely client-side, your notes and text documents remain completely under your control.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Unlike standard text platforms, we do not require any sign-up, email configuration, or account login. Your written content is never sent to remote servers, hosted databases, or network infrastructure. Everything stays entirely private within your local terminal environment.

2. Local Browser Storage

Our auto-save engine utilizes your browser's local storage cache to protect your input against accidental session loss or tab closure. This stored script configuration remains structural to your offline client file layer and can be cleared instantly by using the "Clear Editor" action button or emptying your web browser's application data cache.

This background metadata is strictly utilized to troubleshoot network routing performance and preserve structural web server stability.

4. Third-Party Analytics and Ad Services

We do not work with tracking networks or data monetization brokers. Should our site implement tracking scripts (such as basic visitor counter metrics) or network optimization tools, those elements analyze strictly aggregated technical behavior records without binding tracking identities back to your personal data profiles.

5. Your Rights and Management Options

Since we do not compile user identity tables or store text logs externally on database networks, we cannot supply, update, or remove personal content logs upon direct message request. You maintain absolute direct operational control over your structural tracking configuration via standard browser cookie reset controls and localized data sweep protocols.
